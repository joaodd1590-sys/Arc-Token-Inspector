How is the risk status calculated?

  • Trusted Verified or long-lived contract, healthy number of holders, no obvious abuse functions.
  • Unknown New token or very few holders. Not necessarily malicious, but treat with caution.
  • Risky Contract not verified, suspicious functions (limitless mint, blacklist, pause) or strange patterns on-chain.
